Addiction Recovery Support

Confidential, judgment-free support for your recovery journey

Your Privacy is Protected

All addiction recovery services are strictly confidential. We follow HIPAA guidelines and university policies to ensure your privacy and academic standing are protected.

Recovery is Possible

Addiction affects many college students, and seeking help is a courageous first step. Our comprehensive recovery support program provides evidence-based treatment, peer support, and practical resources to help you achieve and maintain sobriety while pursuing your education.

Crisis & Relapse Prevention Plan

Warning Signs

  • Increased stress or anxiety
  • Social isolation
  • Romanticizing past substance use
  • Neglecting self-care
  • Stopping recovery activities

Emergency Contacts

Campus Crisis Line (555) 123-4567
Addiction Helpline 1-800-662-HELP
Crisis Text Line Text HOME to 741741

Immediate Actions

  1. Remove yourself from triggering situations
  2. Contact your support person immediately
  3. Attend a support meeting (in-person or virtual)
  4. Use healthy coping strategies from your toolkit
  5. If in immediate danger, call emergency services
