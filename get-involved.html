Current Volunteer Opportunities

Peer Wellness Educator

5-10 hours/week

Lead wellness workshops, provide peer support, and help develop educational content for fellow students.

Requirements:

  • Current enrolled student (GPA 2.5+)
  • Interest in health and wellness
  • Strong communication skills
  • Complete 20-hour training program

Benefits:

  • Leadership development training
  • Certificate of completion
  • Reference letters
  • Scholarship opportunities

Event Support Volunteer

Flexible

Help with setup, registration, and coordination of wellness events and workshops.

Requirements:

  • Reliable and punctual
  • Friendly and approachable
  • Available for weekend events
  • Basic orientation required

Benefits:

  • Community service hours
  • Free access to workshops
  • Networking opportunities
  • Flexible scheduling

Digital Content Creator

3-5 hours/week

Create social media content, blog posts, and digital resources to promote wellness awareness.

Requirements:

  • Experience with social media platforms
  • Basic graphic design skills
  • Creative writing ability
  • Understanding of wellness topics

Benefits:

  • Portfolio development
  • Digital marketing experience
  • Creative freedom
  • Professional references
