Sleep Meditation Audio

Guided meditation to help improve sleep quality and establish healthy sleep routines

Best experienced with headphones or earbuds

About This Meditation

This 20-minute guided sleep meditation is designed specifically for students who struggle with racing thoughts, study stress, or irregular sleep schedules. The session uses progressive muscle relaxation and gentle breathing techniques to prepare your mind and body for restful sleep.

Benefits:

  • Reduces pre-sleep anxiety
  • Calms an overactive mind
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Helps establish a bedtime routine

How to Use

  1. Find a comfortable position in bed
  2. Dim or turn off lights
  3. Put on headphones (optional)
  4. Close your eyes and follow along
  5. Don't worry if you fall asleep mid-session

Duration: 20 minutes
Voice: Dr. Maria Rodriguez
Background: Gentle nature sounds

Sleep Hygiene Tips

Before Bed
  • Stop studying 1 hour before sleep
  • Avoid screens 30 minutes before
  • Keep room cool (65-68Â°F)

During Day
  • Get natural sunlight exposure
  • Limit caffeine after 2 PM

  • Take short power naps (20 min max)

Sleep Issues?

If you're experiencing persistent sleep problems, insomnia, or sleep disorders, consider reaching out to campus health services. Chronic sleep issues can significantly impact academic performance and mental health.
