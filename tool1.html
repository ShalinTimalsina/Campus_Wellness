Smart Study Tips

Time Management

  • Use the Pomodoro Technique (25-min focused sessions)
  • Take 5-minute breaks between sessions
  • Schedule challenging subjects during peak hours
  • Build buffer time for unexpected delays

Learning Optimization

  • Review material within 24 hours of learning
  • Use active recall instead of passive reading
  • Create visual mind maps for complex topics
  • Teach concepts to others to reinforce understanding

Wellness Balance

  • Include regular breaks for meals and hydration
  • Schedule physical activity between study blocks
  • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • Plan social activities to prevent burnout
